DockerCon 2019: Opensource Summit Serverless

May 6, 2019 in TALKS
serverless containers opensource dockercon docker
2 min read

I had the opportunity to present and host the Opensource Summit Serverless session along with panelists Burton Rheutan & Pav Jimanov representing OpenFaaS and Yaron Haviv representing Nuclio, at DockerCon 2019. Humbled to be part of the show.

I had a blast at DockerCon 2019 in San Fransisco. It was so much fun!

#DockerCon #OpenSource serverless frameworks panel hosted by @rupakg @_burtonr @PavJimanov on @openfaas focused on portability, ease of use and dev community@yaronhaviv on @nuclioio focused on performance, data & ML use cases@alexellisuk we missed you! pic.twitter.com/TeNhqkUMet

— chanezon (@chanezon) May 3, 2019

At the Opensource Summit: Serverless session, using OpenFaaS and Nuclio, panelists presented use cases and demos, to highlight on-prem usage of serverless & containerization technologies and showcase how they are complementary technologies. The Q&A and roundtable that followed was very engaging, and shed light into many intruiging situations and use cases from the audience.

#OpenSourceSummit @DockerCon : @yaronhaviv showing us how @nuclioio #serverless framework complements #containerization and has use cases more than as an HTTP trigger pic.twitter.com/m8xfAj3TT6

— Hackerspace Mumbai (@hackmum) May 2, 2019

The video for the session is out. Please watch it and let me know your feedback in the comments below.

Thanks a lot to the panelists Burton Rheutan & Pav Jimanov representing OpenFaaS and Yaron Haviv representing Nuclio. It was a blast!
