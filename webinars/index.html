How to Build a Regionally Distributed Serverless Architecture

In this webinar, Alex Casalboni, Senior Software Engineer at Cloud Academy and Rupak Ganguly, Enterprise Solutions Architect at Serverless Inc. explore the architectural advantages of globally distributed serverless applications through a live demo.
